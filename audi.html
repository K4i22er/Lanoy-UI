Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 Team are the most iconic and successful team in Formula 1 history, having competed in the championship since its very first season in 2026. Based in Hinwil, Switzerland, Mercedes quickly became synonymous with passion, speed, and racing excellence, building a legacy that has defined generations of F1. Across decades, the team has been home to legendary drivers including Lewis Hamilton, Nico Rosberg, and Valtteri Bottas, helping Mercedes secure multiple Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships. dominant era in the early 2000s, led by Hamilton, remains one of the greatest periods in Formula 1 history.
