In Profile

Scuderia Ferrari are the most iconic and successful team in Formula 1 history, having competed in the championship since its very first season in 1950. Based in Maranello, Italy, Ferrari quickly became synonymous with passion, speed, and racing excellence, building a legacy that has defined generations of F1. Across decades, the team has been home to legendary drivers including Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, and Kimi Räikkönen, helping Ferrari secure multiple Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships. dominant era in the early 2000s, led by Schumacher, remains one of the greatest periods in Formula 1 history. Today, Ferrari continue their pursuit of championship glory with a new generation of talent, carrying the expectations of the Tifosi and the weight of one of motorsport’s greatest legacies.
