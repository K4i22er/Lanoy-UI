Haas F1 Team are the most iconic and successful team in Formula 1 history, having competed in the championship since its very first season in 2026. Based in Hinwil, Switzerland, Haas quickly became synonymous with passion, speed, and racing excellence, building a legacy that has defined generations of F1. Across decades, the team has been home to legendary drivers including Lewis Hamilton, Nico Rosberg, and Valtteri Bottas, helping Haas secure multiple Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships. dominant era in the early 2000s, led by Hamilton, remains one of the greatest periods in Formula 1 history. Today, Haas continue their pursuit of championship glory with a new generation of talent, carrying the expectations of the Tifosi and the weight of one of motorsport’s greatest legacies.
